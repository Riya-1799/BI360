# Atliq Hardware i360 Business Insights Dashboard (Power BI)  

This repository contains a comprehensive **Business Insights Dashboard** for Atliq Hardware, built using **Power BI**. The project provides actionable insights across **Finance, Sales, Supply Chain, Marketing, and Executive performance** using **DAX measures, KPIs, and interactive data visualizations**.  

🔗 **Live Dashboard**: [View Here](https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJrIjoiMTMzNDlhZDEtYTBiNC00ZmJjLWE0ZTEtZmU4ZjM2NDgwYmE4IiwidCI6ImM2ZTU0OWIzLTVmNDUtNDAzMi1hYWU5LWQ0MjQ0ZGM1YjJjNCJ9)

## 🚀 Features  

✅ **Finance Analytics** – End-to-end P&L, revenue, margin, and profitability tracking.  
✅ **Sales Performance** – Customer and product contribution analysis with top/bottom performers.  
✅ **Supply Chain Insights** – Forecast accuracy, net error %, regional demand-supply balance.  
✅ **Marketing & Market Share** – Channel performance and market share trends across regions.  
✅ **Executive View** – A unified high-level dashboard summarizing KPIs for decision-making.  
✅ **DAX-Powered Modeling** – Custom measures for YoY growth, benchmark vs. actuals, and KPI tracking.  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics & KPIs  

### 🔹 Finance Dashboard  
- **Net Sales ($3.34B in 2022 EST)** – Total revenue after deductions.  
- **Gross Margin % (31.53%)** – Profitability after deducting cost of goods sold.  
- **Net Profit % (-20.68%)** – Profitability after all expenses; highlights losses.  
- **P&L Statement** – Gross Sales, Deductions, Net Sales, COGS, Opex, Net Profit.  
- **YoY & Benchmark Analysis** – Compare current vs. previous year and vs. targets.  

### 🔹 Sales Dashboard  
- **Customer Contribution** – Net Sales & Gross Margin by customers (Amazon, Flipkart, Atliq Exclusive, etc.).  
- **Product Performance** – Revenue and profitability by product categories (Notebooks, Accessories, Desktops, etc.).  
- **Top/Bottom Products & Customers** – Identify best and worst performers.  
- **Regional Sales Distribution** – Sales contribution across global geographies.  

### 🔹 Supply Chain Dashboard  
- **Forecast Accuracy % (81.17%)** – Accuracy of demand forecasting vs. actuals.  
- **Net Error %** – Percentage difference between forecast and sales.  
- **Inventory Efficiency** – Supply-demand balancing and stock movement.  
- **Regional & Subzone Performance** – Supply chain effectiveness across India, NA, LATAM, EU, APAC.  

### 🔹 Marketing & Executive Dashboard  
- **Market Share % by Region** – Company’s share vs competitors.  
- **Revenue by Channel** – Contribution from Retailer, Distributor, Direct.  
- **YoY Growth Trends** – Revenue, Gross Margin %, Net Profit %, Market Share %.  
- **Executive Summary** – Unified high-level KPIs for quick leadership decisions.

## 📂 Data Sources  

- **fact_sales_est** – Sales estimates dataset.  
- **fact_actual_sales** – Actual sales performance.  
- **fact_forecast** – Forecasted demand and revenue.  
- **dim_customer** – Customer details and segmentation.  
- **dim_product** – Product category, subcategory, and classification.  
- **dim_date** – Calendar table for time-based analysis.  
- **dim_market** – Region, subzone, and market details.

## 🎯 Purpose
