# Atliq Hardware i360 Business Insights Dashboard (Power BI)  

This repository contains a comprehensive **Business Insights Dashboard** for Atliq Hardware, built using **Power BI**. The project provides valuable insights into **Supply Chain, Sales, Marketing, Finance, and Executive performance** using **DAX measures and interactive data visualization**.  

🔗 **Live Dashboard**: [View Here](your_powerbi_link_here)  

---

## 🚀 Features  

✅ **Supply Chain Analytics** – Monitor forecast accuracy, demand-supply gaps, and inventory management to reduce absolute error.  
✅ **Marketing Insights** – Analyze product performance, market share trends, and campaign effectiveness.  
✅ **Sales Performance** – Track revenue growth, regional sales, product-level contribution, and customer demand.  
✅ **Finance Overview** – Evaluate profit margins, P&L changes, and financial health for sustainable growth.  
✅ **Executive View** – Consolidated KPIs for leadership to make faster, data-driven decisions.  
✅ **DAX-Powered Modeling** – Advanced measures, KPIs, and aggregations for accurate insights.  

---

## 📂 Data Sources  

- **fact_sales** – Contains transaction-level sales data.  
- **fact_forecast** – Holds forecasted demand and planning numbers.  
- **dim_customer** – Customer details for segmentation and regional analysis.  
- **dim_product** – Product-level hierarchy for category analysis.  
- **dim_date** – Date dimension table for time-based comparisons.  

---

## 🎯 Purpose  

This project is designed for **business leaders, analysts, and managers** who want to:  
- Gain **end-to-end visibility** across sales, supply chain, marketing, and finance.  
- Optimize **strategic decision-making** with real-time KPIs.  
- Identify **risks, growth opportunities, and efficiency gaps** in operations.
